PORTFOLIO SITE

Welcome to the portfolio. You’ve actually been here since you entered the page. Everything you see here is part of the portfolio site.

Much can be said about the site as well as nothing at all.
I’ve chosen to go with a simple approach - create what’s necessary, then add what’s beautiful. On the main page you’ll see not one but two videos. The first is the Hero Video, an effect I could achieve by photoshopping one of my existing images to the desired outcome and then editing it together in Premiere Pro.
Below is another video. A compilation of work done throughout the year 2021 as well as a few overlays. It adds to the atmosphere of the page.

I’ve chosen a black, red and white theme in an attempt to drag you in. To generate an emotion if you will. This isn’t a word-heavy site, the fact that you’re even reading this is surprising, because the visual effect of the website is much more important than the written part of it.
White reflects light, so white letters can be a strain on the eyes to read. This is why all letters on a black background aren’t entirely white but actually the color ‘whitesmoke’.
