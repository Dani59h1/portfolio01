GULDBERG 29

To make this video we first decided on a theme. Once we had all agreed on Brooklyn-99, the next step was to assign different roles to each person and create a storyboard that included two takes of each person and an ending frame. With attention to detail we included appropriate costumes.

The whole thing was then edited in Premiere Pro including freeze frames and colorful overlays to match the Brooklyn-99 style. Thus Guldberg-29 was born.

Corruption had it that we would score either second or third place in all three categories in the competition.
