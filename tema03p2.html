BASIC UX PITCH

The pitch was designed having a sales pitch in mind. First the research was presented. Both from the survey, interview and knowledge gathered from external sites. This explained why Berserk Rebellion became what it is, a street fashion style with sustainability in mind.

It explained our targeted audience, showed our moodboard and styletile and how we’ve implemented those in the final design.

The pitch then ends with what the future would hold for Berserk Rebellion had it been a real brand. Looking at it from a marketing perspective the next step would be to create a community and achieve a growth in loyal customers and recognition.
Design-wise it would consist of refining the site to have it be more user friendly and remove everything unnecessary. Another few ‘think-aloud tests’ of the site would’ve been necessary.
