DANIEL VASILJEVIC

Daniel Vasiljevic is an actor, photographer, videographer and writer born and raised in Copenhagen, Denmark. Here he graduated with a diploma in Acting at Copenhagen Film- and Theatre School in June 2019. Not long after he went to Melbourne, Australia, where he continued his studies in Film Acting for another four months at The Film Space. He's acted in plays under directors such as Bo Madvig, Jeremy Thomas Poulsen, Barry McKenna, Anja Owe and Stuart Lynch.

However, Daniel's passion for film extends as far back as he can remember. He'd always been fascinated by how so many people were able to come together on a movie set to create such art. At the same time his desire to entertain pushed him towards acting at a very young age, and just a few years ago he added directing to his repertoire. Although he enjoys both aspects very much, he always prefers to keep acting and directing separate, so that he can fully devote himself to one craft at a time.

He's since moved back to Copenhagen where he continues to work with film and photography, as well as studying multimedia design at KEA adding to his repertoire of creative outlets.
